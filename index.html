FAQ
Stream not loading?
Ensure a stable internet connection. Some links may be geo-restricted or temporarily offline.
How to add favorites?
Tap the heart icon on any channel. Favorites are saved locally on your device.
Video looks stretched?
Use the Fit button in player controls to toggle between Contain and Cover modes.
